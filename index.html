My mother recently had a fall and the Occupational Therapist is due to come to her home. What are the most important questions I should ask?
I would first encourage them to spend some time really getting to know your relative. Too often they will turn up and do their assessment without thoroughly understanding the nuances of a particular person.

Only then will they start to notice how your mother makes her tea or whether simple things like opening the fridge door have become difficult.
